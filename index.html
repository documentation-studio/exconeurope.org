EX//CON 2026The Experience Conference

UX. DX. Docs. Design Systems. Experience Engineering. Where they all come together — in one online-first event built for makers, not marketers.

EX//CON is a online-first, community-driven conference focused on the craft of product experience. And yes — it actually happens in a European timezone.

The first edition launches in Q3 2026. Chill vibes, killer talks, and a Twitch afterparty with a live DJ set.

  • Lightning talks and roundtable discussions
